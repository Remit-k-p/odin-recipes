Indian Lassi

Back to Home Page

Description

Lassi is a traditional Indian yogurt-based drink that can be sweet or savory. This recipe features a refreshing mango lassi, perfect for hot days.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup mango pulp (fresh or canned)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine yogurt, milk, mango pulp, sugar, and cardamom powder.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. If desired, add ice cubes and blend again.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness if necessary.
  4. Pour into glasses and serve chilled.
