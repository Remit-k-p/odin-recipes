Palak Paneer

Back to Home Page

Description

Palak paneer is a popular Indian dish made with spinach and paneer cheese. It's a delicious and nutritious vegetarian option.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh spinach
  • 8 ounces paneer cheese, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 cup cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blanch the spinach in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then transfer to ice water. Drain and blend into a smooth paste.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add the spinach paste, garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add paneer cubes and cook for another 5 minutes. If using cream, stir it in at this stage.
  6. Serve hot with naan or rice.
